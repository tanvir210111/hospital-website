About Daffodil Hospital

At Daffodil Hospital, our mission is to provide exceptional healthcare services that combine state-of-the-art medical technology with genuine compassion. We understand that every patient is unique, and we strive to deliver personalized care tailored to each individual’s needs. Our hospital is a trusted destination for patients seeking advanced treatments, a comfortable environment, and a team that truly cares.

Our Facilities

Modern Inpatient & Outpatient Services

Our hospital offers well-equipped inpatient rooms and outpatient clinics designed for comfort, safety, and privacy. Whether you require short-term observation or longer stays, our facilities are structured to support your healing process with the utmost care.

24/7 Emergency Care

Emergencies don’t wait, and neither do we. Our round-the-clock emergency department is staffed by highly trained medical professionals ready to provide immediate attention. With advanced life-support equipment and streamlined protocols, we ensure rapid and effective treatment in critical situations.

Specialized Departments

  • Cardiology: Comprehensive heart care including diagnostics, interventions, and rehabilitation.
  • Neurology: Treatment of neurological disorders such as stroke, epilepsy, and neurodegenerative diseases.
  • Orthopedics: Care for bone, joint, and muscular conditions including surgeries and physical therapies.
  • Pediatrics: Full spectrum child healthcare from newborns to adolescents.
  • Oncology: Advanced cancer diagnosis, chemotherapy, radiation, and supportive care.
  • Gynecology & Obstetrics: Women’s health, pregnancy care, and childbirth services.
  • Dermatology: Diagnosis and treatment of skin conditions and diseases.
  • Gastroenterology: Care for digestive system disorders including endoscopy services.
  • Pulmonology: Treatment of respiratory diseases like asthma, COPD, and lung infections.
  • Nephrology: Kidney health, dialysis services, and treatment of renal disorders.
  • Endocrinology: Management of hormonal disorders including diabetes and thyroid conditions.
  • Urology: Urinary tract and male reproductive health services.
  • ENT (Ear, Nose & Throat): Diagnosis and treatment of head and neck conditions.
  • Ophthalmology: Eye care services including surgeries, diagnostics, and vision correction.
  • Psychiatry & Mental Health: Counseling, therapy, and psychiatric care.
  • Physical Medicine & Rehabilitation: Support for post-injury and chronic condition recovery.
  • Anesthesiology: Pain management and anesthesia services for surgical procedures.

Diagnostic Services

  • Laboratory Testing: Comprehensive blood tests, pathology, microbiology, and biochemistry with rapid turnaround.
  • Digital X-Ray: High-resolution imaging for bones and chest.
  • Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI): Detailed scans for soft tissue, brain, spine, joints, and organs.
  • Computed Tomography (CT) Scan: Cross-sectional imaging for trauma, tumors, and complex diagnoses.
  • Ultrasound: Non-invasive imaging for abdomen, pelvic, cardiac, and vascular studies.
  • Electrocardiogram (ECG/EKG): Heart electrical activity monitoring.
  • Echocardiography: Ultrasound of the heart to assess function and structure.
  • Pulmonary Function Tests: Assess lung capacity and breathing issues.
  • Endoscopy & Colonoscopy: Visual examination of the digestive tract.
  • Mammography: Breast cancer screening.
  • Bone Densitometry: Assessment for osteoporosis.
  • Neurological Studies: EEG and nerve conduction tests for brain and nerve function.

Surgical Suites

Our operating rooms are equipped with the latest technology to support a wide range of surgical procedures. We maintain strict standards of sterility and safety, ensuring every surgery is conducted efficiently with the patient’s well-being as our priority.

Pharmacy & Medication Management

Convenient access to medications is vital for recovery. Our in-house pharmacy provides prescribed medicines and expert counseling to help patients understand their treatments and manage their medications safely.

Rehabilitation & Therapy Services

Healing continues beyond hospital walls. Our rehabilitation center offers physical therapy, occupational therapy, speech therapy, and counseling services designed to support your full recovery and improve quality of life.

Patient Support Services

Beyond clinical care, we offer:

  • Nutritional counseling to promote healthy lifestyles.
  • Social work support to assist with personal and family needs.
  • Educational programs to empower patients with knowledge about their health.

Our healthcare professionals are dedicated to treating every patient with kindness, respect, and empathy.
