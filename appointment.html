At Daffodil Hospital, we offer a wide range of medical services to cater to the diverse health needs of our community. Our state-of-the-art facilities and highly qualified staff ensure that you receive the best possible care.

Specialized Departments

  • Cardiology: Comprehensive heart care including diagnostics, interventions, and rehabilitation.
  • Neurology: Treatment of neurological disorders such as stroke, epilepsy, and neurodegenerative diseases.
  • Orthopedics: Care for bone, joint, and muscular conditions including surgeries and physical therapies.
  • Pediatrics: Full spectrum child healthcare from newborns to adolescents.
  • Oncology: Advanced cancer diagnosis, chemotherapy, radiation, and supportive care.

  • Dermatology: Diagnosis and treatment of skin conditions and diseases.
  • Gastroenterology: Care for digestive system disorders including endoscopy services.
  • Pulmonology: Treatment of respiratory diseases like asthma, COPD, and lung infections.
  • Nephrology: Kidney health, dialysis services, and treatment of renal disorders.
  • Endocrinology: Management of hormonal disorders including diabetes and thyroid conditions.
  • Urology: Urinary tract and male reproductive health services.
  • ENT (Ear, Nose & Throat): Diagnosis and treatment of head and neck conditions.
  • Ophthalmology: Eye care services including surgeries, diagnostics, and vision correction.
  • Psychiatry & Mental Health: Counseling, therapy, and psychiatric care.
  • Physical Medicine & Rehabilitation: Support for post-injury and chronic condition recovery.
  • Anesthesiology: Pain management and anesthesia services for surgical procedures.

Diagnostic Services

  • Laboratory Testing: Comprehensive blood tests, pathology, microbiology, and biochemistry with rapid turnaround.
  • Digital X-Ray: High-resolution imaging for bones and chest.
  • Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI): Detailed scans for soft tissue, brain, spine, joints, and organs.
  • Computed Tomography (CT) Scan: Cross-sectional imaging for trauma, tumors, and complex diagnoses.
  • Ultrasound: Non-invasive imaging for abdomen, pelvic, cardiac, and vascular studies.
  • Electrocardiogram (ECG/EKG): Heart electrical activity monitoring.
  • Echocardiography: Ultrasound of the heart to assess function and structure.
  • Pulmonary Function Tests: Assess lung capacity and breathing issues.
  • Endoscopy & Colonoscopy: Visual examination of the digestive tract.
  • Mammography: Breast cancer screening.
  • Bone Densitometry: Assessment for osteoporosis.
  • Neurological Studies: EEG and nerve conduction tests for brain and nerve function.
